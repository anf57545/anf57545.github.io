Anne's Guide to Louisville

Hi, my name is Anne Friedman. Four years ago, I moved from Louisville, Kentucky to Athens, Geogia for school. While it feels crazy that four years have passed by, I am about to graduate. After graduation I will be working at a Bourbon Distillery about on hour south of Louisville. Since there is very little to do in that town, Bardstown, I will be commuting. Living in Athens has been great, but I am so excited though to move back to Louisville. I cannot wait to do all the things I have missed over the last four years, plus a couple new things that I cannot wait to try. With this webpage I want to share what I believe are the best parts of Louisville!
